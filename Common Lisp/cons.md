## function
[cons](http://clhs.lisp.se/Body/f_cons.htm) as a function creates a new cons (class). You can give it two objects and it will combine those where object 1 is the [[car]] and object 2 is [[cdr]] like (o1 . o2) .
## class
[cons](http://clhs.lisp.se/Body/t_cons.htm) as a class is one of the main data structures in lisp. Its made of a car and cdr, and just has those two slots.
