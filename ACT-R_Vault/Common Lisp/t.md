[t](https://www.lispworks.com/documentation/HyperSpec/Body/v_t.htm#t) is a [[constant value]] that represents True. 
You do not need to use t, since anything other than [[nil]] can also represent true. In ACT-R, other values are often returned instead of t, which are sometimes used but often just ignored.
